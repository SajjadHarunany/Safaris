Why Kenya?

From the Great Migration in Maasai Mara to the Big Five in Tsavo and the birdlife-rich shores of Lake Nakuru—Kenya delivers world-class safari adventures.

Top Kenyan Destinations

Masai Mara

Big cats, dramatic landscapes, and the famous wildebeest migration.

Meru National Park

Wildlife-rich forests and sweeping savannah scenes.

Lake Nakuru

Flamingos, rhinos and exceptional birdwatching.

Tsavo West

Big tuskers, red earth, and legendary wildlife sightings.

Tsavo East

Riverine habitats and a stunning safari backdrop.

Amboseli

Classic views of Kilimanjaro and vast elephant country.

Nairobi National Park

Great wildlife close to the city—perfect for quick safari breaks.

Lake Naivasha

Lake life, boating options, and abundant bird species.

Aberdare

Mist forests, waterfalls, and unforgettable highland wildlife.

Samburu

Distinct northern species and dramatic landscapes.
